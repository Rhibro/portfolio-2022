About

Born and raised in Toronto, Canada. I have adventured all over this wide world and have now found myself ready to settle in Malmö, Sweden. I will be coming to the end of my studies at Sundsgården Folkhögskola in Front-end Development mid-January 2023. My education has given me many tools such as the ability to create fully responsive web-pages, a keen eye for accessibility, and an in-depth fluency in HTML, CSS, SCSS, and JavaScript. My extensive background in Theatre and Specialty Coffee has allowed me to develop my skills in working with large groups, taking on leadership roles, while also giving and receiving constructive criticism. I look forward to working with a company where I am given new challenges everyday and a supportive work environment where we can learn from each other.
